Welcome to CSSS 508: Introduction to R for Social Scientists

University of Washington, Spring 2016

Instructor: Rebecca Ferrell, PhD student, UW Statistics

Canvas page for course (enrolled students only)

Helpful resources:
  • R for Data Science textbook draft by Garrett Grolemund and Hadley Wickham. Some chapters not complete yet but this is the best overall text I can recommend at this time.
  • Jenny Bryan's materials for STAT545 at UBC. My class is an abbreviated rip-off of her class, so more explanations can be found on her page.
  • Instructions for using RStudio in the CSSCR lab (hopefully week 1 only)
